Known as the "King of Wild Greens," Dureup is a rare delicacy that signals the awakening of the Korean landscape. In Namhae, these shoots are hand-plucked from coastal mountain slopes where the sea breeze imparts a unique mineral complexity not found elsewhere.

Preparation & Serving

expand_more

Blanching

Boil in salted water for 1-2 minutes. Rinse immediately in cold water to preserve the vibrant green color and crisp bite.

Tempura

Lightly coat in a thin batter and flash-fry. The heat unlocks a nutty aroma hidden within the woody stem.
